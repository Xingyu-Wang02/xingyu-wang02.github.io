Research

My research focuses on statistical inference, uncertainty quantification, and distribution learning. I am especially interested in finite-sample guarantees, robust methodology, and practical algorithms for modern high-dimensional data.

  • Current direction: Distribution learning and uncertainty quantification.
  • Methodological interests: Nonasymptotic/asymptotic inference and learning theory.
  • Applications: Reliable decision-making under distribution shift.
